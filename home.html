About

gawCTF is designed to be an enjoyable training activity for our Black Falcons, and anyone else interested in cybersecurity. Development for gawCTF began in 2019, with many Black Falcons, now alumni, designing various creative cybersecurity-related challenges. In 2022, development of the gawCTF website began, for launch in the summer of the same year. We hope that everyone has a fun experience with this capture the flag, and good luck!
